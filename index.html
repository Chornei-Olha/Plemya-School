I AM OFTEN ASKED

Why should I choose you?

We not only teach, but also share our practical experience, where we analyze not just how to make pictures, but what a real project is and how to work on it and make money on it.

What is the language of the courses?

Even if you don't speak English fluently, you can still fully participate in our program. We offer support in over 20 languages, including live conferences, recordings, and text-based coaching with translation.

Do I need a powerful computer?

Here is a basic computer configuration for 3D architectural visualization:
- Processor:
Intel Core i7 or AMD Ryzen 7 (or later) with a minimum of 6 cores
- Graphics card:
NVIDIA RTX 2060 SUPER or AMD Radeon RX 5700 XT (or newer) with a minimum of 8 GB VRAM
- RAM:
16 GB or more DDR4 RAM
- Storage:
SSD (solid-state drive) with at least 500 GB of storage for faster boot times
- Operating system:
Windows 10 or 11 (64-bit)
These are the minimum requirements.

Do I need to have previous experience in architecture or design?

No, previous experience in architecture or design is not required. The course is designed to be accessible to individuals with a range of backgrounds. We'll provide the necessary knowledge and skills to create compelling visualizations.

What kind of projects can I expect to work on during the course?

You'll have the opportunity to work on a variety of projects, from creating simple interior scenes to complex exterior visualizations. We'll provide guidance and feedback to help you develop your skills and create high-quality renderings.

Will I be able to communicate with other students during the course?

Absolutely! We have chats where you can communicate, share ideas, ask questions, and even get feedback from each other. It's like a big design party where everyone helps each other grow. üëØ
